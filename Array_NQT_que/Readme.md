Examples: 
Input: arr[] {1,2,3,1,5,5}, k=3
o/p: 3 3 4 5
Explanation:

Maximum among of 2,3,1 is 3
Maximum among of 3,1,4 is 4
Maximum among of 1,4,5 is 5
